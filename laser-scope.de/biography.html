Around 2021, Thomas (drums), Christian (bass) and Moritz (guitar) started rehearsing and writing songs together. The line-up was completed with Jens (guitar) at the end of 2021 and Max (vocals) at the beginning of 2022 - LASER SCOPE was born.
Over the course of the year, more songs were written and EP recordings began in the summer of 2023, followed by the first gig in 2024.
